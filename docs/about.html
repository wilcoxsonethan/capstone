Our Goal

Our goal is to help more people become financially literate and understand the stock market better.

About our project

The Stock Trading Simulator is a versatile web application designed to provide users with a hands-on stock trading experience while serving as an educational resource. The platform, seamlessly integrated with the Alpaca API, offers a plethora of features. Users can register and log in with ease, access real-time stock market news, create personalized watchlists, and engage with AI-driven insights. We offer users access to a vast repository of educational resources focused on trading strategies and market analysis, providing valuable insights and knowledge in these areas. The core feature is an interactive virtual stock market, enabling users to simulate trading using real stock data. Robust buy and sell functionalities allow users to manage their portfolio effectively. Strong security measures and JWT tokens safeguard user data. Future enhancements include advanced trading options, portfolio tracking, and community features. The Stock Trading Simulator is an inclusive platform suitable for aspiring traders, knowledge seekers, and financial educators alike, offering a dynamic environment to learn, practice, and stay informed about the stock market.
